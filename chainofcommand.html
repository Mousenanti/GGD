Chain of Command
And regulation entails organizational effectiveness, a chain of command...—Sun Tzu

The Chain of Command (CoC) ensures smooth operations by requiring cadets to follow proper communication channels, except in emergencies. Cadets report to their respective Platoon Leaders, with leadership maintaining accessibility via multiple platforms. Communications should include relevant personnel and parents for transparency. Senior cadets are expected to check in regularly, and officers meet biweekly to address unit matters.
